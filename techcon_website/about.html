Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meet-up of passionate tech professionals and has since evolved into a global event drawing thousands of attendees every year.

Our Mission

We strive to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech community. TechCon is a platform for sharing knowledge, exploring cutting-edge technologies, and empowering the next generation of tech leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted a diverse lineup of world-renowned speakers who are leaders in their fields:

  • Ada Lovelace – Pioneering computer programmer and visionary in early computing theory.
  • Alan Turing – Father of modern computing and artificial intelligence theorist.
  • Grace Hopper – Renowned computer scientist and naval officer, creator of the first compiler.
